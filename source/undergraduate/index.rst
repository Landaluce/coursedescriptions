Undergraduate Programs
=======================

Welcome to the Undergraduate Programs *informational* site for the Computer Science Department at Loyola University Chicago.

This site is aimed at helping *current* students find detailed information
about the undergraduate programs in the `Computer Science Department`_ at `Loyola University Chicago <https://luc.edu>`_.

.. note::

   See the main `Computer Science Department`_ web site for general program information and how to *apply* for admission to our undergraduate programs.

This site is also available in |pdf-version| and |epub-version| formats.
